Caltech FlyTracker


Requirements:
   64-bit OS X, Windows, or Linux. Matlab R2013b or later (with Image Processing and Statistics toolboxes).

Quick start:

  1. Download and unzip the FlyTracker.
  2. Add FlyTracker to your Matlab path by navigating to its directory and typing:
    >> addpath(genpath(pwd)); savepath;
  3. Collect all videos with identical filming- and experimental conditions into one folder.
  4. Track your videos using the tracker interface (where you will calibrate tracker and specify options):
    >> tracker
  5. View tracking results and annotate videos using the visualizer:   
    >> visualizer
